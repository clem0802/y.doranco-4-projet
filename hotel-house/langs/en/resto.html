RESTAURANT


Our Hotel-House Restaurant has a lunch special "brasserie" in French – tables go fast, so secure your reservation by texting us at +(672) 12 3456 or sending us an email at home@hotelhouse.com. If you’re craving ice cream, make sure to visit Cony Spot just down the block – their hand-churned homemade butter brickle is all kids' personal favorite and won awards for best ice cream in the state fair. You may even see our Activities Director, Georges, helping prepare the fresh-squeezed orange juice before the pool opens at 10 am.
