About me

About Me

Hi, I am Younes REDJDAL, an honest, serious and cool taxi driver in Paris for decades. I know by heart all big and small corners in Paris. I respect all my guests, regardless of each guest's gender, religion, profession, age... No guest had ever been upset with my rides, simply because I am very professional. By the way, I am driving only in Paris and its outskirts, I don't go beyond "la petite couronne".

How I work?

My car's seating capacity is 5, driver included. And I am willing to serve all kinds of clients:
(1) happy tourists who visit or re-visit Paris, the amazing capital city,
(2) fully-scheduled businesswomen and businessmen running up and down,
(3) young & old wedding couples preparing their beautiful weddings,
(4) as well as cancer patients who need regular rides for their treatments.
